how does the metamask wallet function

ans :

MetaMask functions as an Ethereum wallet and browser extension. It lets you:

Create a secure wallet with a password and backup phrase.
View and manage your Ethereum assets.
Send and receive Ether and tokens.
Interact with Ethereum DApps.
Manage your transaction history.
Secure your wallet with a password and backup phrase.
Access and control your assets on the Ethereum blockchain.
